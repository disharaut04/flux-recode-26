# flux-recode-26
A fun browser game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript where you drive a Mumbai rickshaw through traffic, collect coins, avoid crashes, and survive as long as possible.
Authors- Disha Raut & Saloni Sonawane
Project name :
🚕 Mumbai Rickshaw Simulator

Hey! Welcome to Mumbai Rickshaw Simulator 😎

In this game, you drive a rickshaw in Mumbai’s crazy traffic. Your job is simple—collect coins, grab fuel, and don’t crash!

🎮 How to Play
	•	Move Left/Right: Use the << and >> buttons, or arrow keys on your keyboard.
	•	Collect Coins: Coins give you points 🪙
	•	Fuel Packs: Grab them so your rickshaw doesn’t stop 🔵⛽
	•	Avoid Cars: Hitting them ends the game 🚧
	•	Traffic Signals: Cars stop at red lights, so watch the roads!
	•	Rain Mode: Turn on rain for some extra fun 🌧
	•	Pause: Pause anytime with ⏸

🏅 Scoring System
  • Each coin = +10 points
  • Fuel slowly decreases over time
  • Collect fuel packs 🔵
  
🏆 Your Goal

Try to collect as many coins as you can before your fuel runs out or you crash. Can you survive Mumbai traffic like a pro?

🔧 Features
	•	Smooth controls for your rickshaw
	•	Random cars, coins, and fuel packs appear
	•	Traffic lights make it realistic
	•	Rain effect for extra challenge
	•	Game gets harder over time

🔄 Restart

Game over? No problem! Hit Restart and try again. Every run is a new chance to beat your high score!

Enjoy the ride through Mumbai Traffic!🚦🚗
